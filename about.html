My Story

I graduated from UMASS Amherst with a BS in Human Nutrition. Eager to start making money and move to the city after graduation I took an administrative job at Boston Children’s Hospital. From there I went on to other administrative work at Accenture (a large consulting firm) and Industrial Defender (a cyber security company). I left Industrial Defender when I had my son in 2006 and began working with a small environmental contractor. I’ve been there for the last 18 years!!

Working in administration is like mastering the art of connecting with others. It’s taught me that success comes from building strong relationships—because when you can collaborate and vibe with your coworkers, there’s no challenge you can’t tackle together!"
